Train Like a Pro. Play With Purpose.

Premier Soccer Training Camp is Charlottes premier player-development academy. We combine UEFA-licensed coaching, sports-science conditioning, and individual film review so every athlete — from 5-year-old first-timers to college-bound seniors — leaves a better, smarter, more confident player.

Our 12-acre facility includes four full-size pitches, two indoor turfs, a strength and recovery suite, and a video analysis classroom. Sessions run year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you accept?

All current programs serve ages 5 to 18. Adult clinics return in Fall 2026.

Do you offer summer camps?

Yes - full-day (9am-3pm) and half-day options run every week from June 9 through August 15. Lunch included on full-day sessions.

What if my child has never played?

Great - Little Strikers and the intro Skills Academy track are specifically designed for first-timers. We will match them to the right group.

Do you help with college recruiting?

Yes. Our Elite Pathway program includes ID profile creation, college outreach guidance, two regional showcases each year, and one-on-one with our Director of Recruiting.

How do I register?

Use the form on this page or visit our Programs page for current openings. We will respond within one business day.
