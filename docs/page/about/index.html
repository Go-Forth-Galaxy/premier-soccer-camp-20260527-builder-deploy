About

Our Mission

Premier Soccer Training Camp was founded in 2014 by former Sporting CP youth coach Carlos Mendes with a simple belief: every player who walks onto our field deserves world-class instruction, no matter their starting level.

Twelve years later, weve trained 2,400+ players, sent 38 to college rosters, and built one of the Southeasts most respected academies. We do it by keeping ratios tiny (8:1 max), hiring only licensed coaches, and treating every session like a player development laboratory.
